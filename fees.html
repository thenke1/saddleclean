Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge for both pickup and delivery?

No, the $25 fee covers both pickup and delivery within 30 miles. It's a round-trip fee.

Can I schedule same-day service?

Rush service requires 24 hours minimum. Please contact us as early as possible for urgent requests.

What if I'm beyond 30 miles?

We're happy to serve you! The base fee is $25, plus $2 per mile for each mile beyond 30.

How do I calculate my total cost?

Service price + pickup fee (if applicable) + mileage fee (if beyond 30 miles) + rush fee (if needed).
