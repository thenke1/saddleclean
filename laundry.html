Pickup & Delivery

Convenient pickup and drop-off service available. We come to you, pick-up your tack and return it after cleaning and conditioning.

  • Scheduled pickup times
  • Serving within 30 miles
  • Safe and secure transport
  • Contactless options available
  • Text updates on status
  • Blankets must be labeled for each owner and in garbage bags. 10 blanket minimum. $25 additional fee each way. If payment is not received before your drop off date, you will need to pick up your blankets at the farm or pay an additional $25 for second trip to deliver. For biosecurity purposes, employees will not enter the barn premise. Blankets are to be picked up in the parking lot of the facility.
